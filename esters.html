ESTERS

Esters Esters Derived from carboxylic acids containing the -COOH group, and in an ester the hydrogen in this group is replaced by a hydrocarbon group of some kind. are industrial agents used to react with water to produce alcohols and both inorganic and organic acids. They serve as functional products for personal care and industrial applications and are among the value-added products within the Musim Mas portfolio.

They include Esters Esters Derived from carboxylic acids containing the -COOH group, and in an ester the hydrogen in this group is replaced by a hydrocarbon group of some kind. made from:

Fatty Acids such as:

  • Caprylic/Capric Acid

  • Lauric Acid

  • Stearic acid

  • Synthetic acids

Alcohols such as:

  • Isopropyl Alcohol

  • Glycerol

  • 2-Ethylhexyl Alcohol,

  • Cetyl/Stearyl Alcohol,

  • Trimethylolpropane

  • Pentaerythritol

Manufactured under the MASESTER® brand, our products function as emulsifiers Emulsifiers Additives that allow oils to be blended with water, overcoming their natural reluctance to mix. , emollients, opacifiers, viscosity modifiers in personal care, processing aids in polymers, and as lubricants in metalworking fluids

Our product range is also widely used in cosmetic products formulated for body care, skincare, and hair care.

In polymers, they are used as antistatic and antifogging aids and lubricants; in metal working fluids as lubricity improvers and mold release agents.

MASESTER® Functional Products are PHO-free (Partially Hydrogenated Oil), non-GMO, non-allergenic, Kosher, Halal and are derived from high-quality sustainable palm Oil Palm Oil Oil which comes from the fruit of the oil palm tree, Elaeis Guineensis. sourced by Musim Mas group.
