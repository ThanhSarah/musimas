ABOUT MUSIM MAS

Musim Mas Group is one of the leading global palm oil companies with its headquarters in Singapore and global operational presence in 13 countries.

With sustainability as a core value, we were the first company with major operations in Indonesia to join the Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO) in 2004.

We are involved in every part of the palm oil supply chain:

  • Managing oil palm plantations to produce fresh fruit bunches (FFB)
  • Milling FFB to produce Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Kernel (PK)
  • Crushing PK to obtain crude Palm Kernel Oil (PKO)
  • Refining CPO and PKO
  • Further processing to produce value-added products such as Specialty Fats, Oleochemicals, Biodiesel and functional products such as Emulsifiers
  • Manufacturing consumer goods such as cooking oil and personal care products
  • Shipping and merchandising value-added products to global destinations
