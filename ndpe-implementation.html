NDPE IMPLEMENTATION

Implementation Plan

Musim Mas launched our Sustainability Policy in 2014 and has been pro-actively working in close collaboration with technical consultants Daemeter, Proforest and Aidenvironment to advance and monitor our progress in implementing our Sustainability Policy. We published the Sustainability Policy Implementation Plan in 2018 to communicate our strategy to achieve our NDPE vision and goals leading up to 2020 and beyond.

The Implementation Plan outlines our approach towards managing:

  • Our own supply

  • Third-party suppliers

  • Smallholders
