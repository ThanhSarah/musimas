With a strong global presence in 13 countries, we draw on the skills of our extensive, innovative workforce. We source our raw materials from Malaysia and Indonesia, with the latter being our main operational base. We have production plants located across Asia and Europe. Our main research and development (R&D) center is based in Singapore. Such interconnected operations across the globe empower us to deliver quality and safe products while ensuring efficiency throughout the entire spectrum of our business.

Inter-Continental Oils & Fats (ICOF) handles the Group’s global marketing activities. Based in Singapore, ICOF has local offices across Asia, Europe and the United States of America to oversee the marketing and distribution of high-quality palm oils, lauric oils and derivative products to more than 80 countries.
