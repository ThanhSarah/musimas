INTER-CONTINENTAL OILS AND FATS

Inter-Continental Oils and Fats (ICOF) is the one-stop global leader in the merchandising and distribution of palm oil Palm oil Oil which comes from the fruit of the oil palm tree, Elaeis Guineensis. and its derivative products. ICOF is the marketing arm of the Musim Mas Group with a logistics and distribution network to supplement our offering.
